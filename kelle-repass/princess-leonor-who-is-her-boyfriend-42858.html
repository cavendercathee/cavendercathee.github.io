The oldest child of King Felipe VI and Queen Letizia and the putative heir to the Spanish throne is Leonor, Princess of Asturias. Leonor de Todos los Santos de Borbón y Ortiz is her full name.

In addition to her official title as Princess of Asturias, she also holds the historical titles of Princess of Girona, Princess of Viana, Duchess of Montblanc, Countess of Cervera, and Lady of Balaguer.

These ages-old titles were given to her by her ancestors. Leonor would be Spain’s first queen regnant since Isabella II, who ruled from 1833 to 1868, if she were to succeed in ascending to the throne.

Bio of Princess Leonor

Princess Leonor turned 17 on October 31, 2005, making her now. Due to her leadership of the Princess of Girona Foundation and the Princess of Asturias Foundation, Princess Leonor has tremendous prominence.
