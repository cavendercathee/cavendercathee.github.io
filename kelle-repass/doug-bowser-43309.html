Biography

Doug Bowser was born in Danville, CA on August 11, 1965. A business executive who has risen to fame as the president of Nintendo of America. In February 2019, he was announced as the heir apparent to become Nintendo’s COO and president in April 2019. He was announced as Nintendo America’s impending new COO and president to succeed Reggie Fils-Aime. He graduated from the University of Utah in 1984 with his BA in organizational communication and business. He subsequently worked in sales for Procter & Gamble for ten years.
