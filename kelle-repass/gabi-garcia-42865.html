Biography

Gabi Garcia was born in Rio Grande Do Sul, Brazil on November 17, 1985. Brazilian MMA fighter with many gold medals to her name as she is a ten-time Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu world champion and a six-time Panamerican champion. Her first MMA fight was against Lei’D Tapa in December of 2015 where she won in the first round by TKO. In 2013 she appeared on The Ultimate Fighter: Brazil 3 as a guest coach for Wanderlei Silva. She won her first World Championship for Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in 2008.
