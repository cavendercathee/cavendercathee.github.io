An English comedian, performer, director, and writer by the name of Ricky Gervais. His most well-known work is The Office, a British television mockumentary sitcom that he co-created, co-wrote, and starred in.

What is the Net Worth of Ricky Gervais? Salary, Earnings

As of February 2023, British-born actor, comedian, writer, and filmmaker Ricky has a net worth of $14 million. He gained the vast majority of his wealth from creating the television program The Office.

Where was Ricky Gervais born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

On June 25, 1961, Ricky Dene Gervais was born in Reading, England. His father, Franco-Ontarian Lawrence Raymond Gervais, was a foreign serviceman who fought in England during World War II. His mother was an English homemaker named Eva Sophia, née House. Ricky was the youngest of his parents’ four children, who also included an older sister named Marsha, two siblings named Larry and Robert, and Ricky.

He attended Whitney Infant School when he first began school before transferring to Whitney Junior School. Before starting at the University of London, Ricky Gervais graduated from University College London with an upper second-class honors degree in philosophy. But he never received his diploma from there.

Is Ricky Gervais Single? Relationship

Since 1984, Ricky has shared a home with Jane Fallon, a renowned producer,, and screenwriter he first encountered in 1982 while attending University College London. The couple divides their time between New York City and Hampstead.

According to atheist Gervais, they did not get married because there is no point in performing a “ceremony before the eyes of God because there is no God.” Additionally, the couple opted against having kids.
