Biography

Jonny Wickersham was born in California on May 26, 1968. Best known for his work with the Fullerton, California-based punk band Social Distortion, this guitarist also performed with The Cadillac Tramps, Youth Brigade, and U.S. Bombs and recorded a solo album titled Salvation Town. He and vocalist Mike Ness were both members of Social Distortion. He first performed with Social Distortion in 2000 during a concert in honor of his childhood friend and the band’s recently deceased guitarist, Dennis Danell.
