Call Me Chihiro Is Set To Premiere On Netflix

Publish date: 2024-05-04

The world premiere of Netflix’s newest Japanese drama film, Call Me Chihiro, is expected to take place on the streaming service on Thursday, February 23, 2023, at 3 a.m. Eastern Time (ET).

The film’s main character is a woman who used to be a prostitute and is now employed at a bento stand, where she develops meaningful relationships with many of the customers. In the film Call Me Chihiro, Kasumi Arimura plays the main character Chihiro. A number of other actors and actresses play important supporting roles. Rikiya Imaizumi is in charge of the film’s direction.

Call Me Chihiro on Netflix: A look at the trailer, the story, and what viewers can expect, as well as additional information

Netflix released the official trailer for their show “Call Me Chihiro” on December 13, 2022. It foreshadows the many significant events that will occur in the life of the heroine Chihiro, who used to be a prostitute but is now employed at a bento stand.

With her new job, she quickly makes friends with a large number of clients, and she plays an important role in changing their worldviews and attitudes toward life. The concept of the film is explained in the opening voiceover heard at the beginning of the trailer:

Chihiro now works at a local bento shop after previously working as a prostitute. The preview gives a sense of the film’s pleasant atmosphere by summarizing and presenting various touching events from Chihiro’s life in a condensed format. Along with the teaser, Netflix has released the official description for the film on its YouTube account. The following is the synopsis:

The world would be a different place if you met her. Chihiro is a woman who previously worked in the adult entertainment industry and is now employed at a small bento shop in a coastal town. In this section, she discusses the importance of living one’s life according to one’s own preferences in an era when everyone is dealing with some kind of difficulty. Kasumi Arimura stars in this remarkable story.”

According to the trailer and plot summary, audiences can expect a story that is both emotionally engaging and conceptually deep, and that delves into a variety of intriguing topics such as the will to live, happiness, and many more.

An introduction to the Call Me Chihiro cast

Kasumi Arimura plays the main character, Chihiro, in the recently released Netflix drama film. Chihiro is a cheerful and exciting lady, but what really sets her apart is the melancholy streak that runs through her veins. She is the heart and soul of the film, and her journey is at the heart of the emotional impact it has on its audience.

In addition to her role in the anime series Call Me Chihiro, Kasumi Arimura has appeared in a number of well-known and critically acclaimed films and television shows. To name a few, these films and shows are Phases of the Moon, Zenkamono, Prior Convictions, and Sensuikan Cappellini-gou no bouken.

Lily Franky, who plays Utsumi, is an actress who co-stars with Kasumi Arimura Arimura and has a significant role in the show. Despite the fact that information on Utsumi is being kept under wraps for the time being, fans can expect Franky to play a significant role in the film. Lily Franky’s notable film and television roles include The Makanai: Cooking for the Maiko House, He’s Expecting, Our Little Sister, and Like Father, Like Son, among many others.

Toshie Negishi, who plays Nagai, Mitsuri Hirata, who plays Bito, and Ryûya Wakaba, who plays Taniguchi are among the supporting actors. There are numerous other outstanding actors. Netflix will begin streaming the anime series Call Me Chihiro on Thursday, February 23, 2023, so don’t miss out.
